We had discussed the interpretation of Varela's rules at our last meeting.
By studying the displays in Fig 2 of the bittorio paper, I am pretty
sure I understand them now.

The rules are 8 bit numbers. The left-most 4 bits are the output when
the cell's current state is '1', the right-most 4 bits are the outputs
when the cell's current state is '0'. With in each 4-bit byte, the bits
represent the output when the input is:

00, 01, 10, 11

in that order, left to right.
